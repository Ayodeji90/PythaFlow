## The problem, in one line

Every week, guests message and call your venue **after your team clocks out** —
and every unanswered enquiry is a booking that goes to the place down the street.

## What the concierge does

- **Answers every guest instantly, 24/7** — on your website chat and WhatsApp —
  in the guest's own language.
- **Answers only from *your* facts.** We load your menu, hours, and policies; it
  never invents prices or promises. If it doesn't know, it says a team member
  will follow up — and flags your staff.
- **Takes reservation requests** and drafts them for your team. **Nothing is
  confirmed without a human's approval.** Your staff stays in charge, always.
- **Hands off gracefully** — a guest who asks for a person gets a person.
