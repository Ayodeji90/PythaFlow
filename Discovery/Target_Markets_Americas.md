### Suggested First‑Wave Pilot
1. **United States** – start with a handful of independent restaurants in major tourism hubs (e.g., Miami, New Orleans, San Diego).<br>2. **Canada** – parallel pilot in Toronto/Vancouver to validate cross‑border pricing.
3. **Bahamas & Barbados** – quick‑win pilots with 5‑10 restaurants each to generate early case studies.
4. **Cayman Islands** – ultra‑small pilot (3‑5 venues) to test premium pricing.
