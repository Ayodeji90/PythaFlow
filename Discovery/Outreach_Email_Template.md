# Outreach Templates — v2 (Concierge positioning)

*Replaces the v1 platform-era template (waste forecasting / dynamic pricing —
retired with that positioning). One pain, one proof, one ask. Under 120 words.*

**Rules:** personalize the first line for every send (their rating, a review
quote, a dish) — never send unpersonalized. No "AI platform" language. Sell the
outcome (recovered bookings), not the technology. Only promise what's built:
**text concierge (web chat + WhatsApp)** — no voice claims yet.

---
