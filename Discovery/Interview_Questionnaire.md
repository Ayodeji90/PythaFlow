# Restaurant Owner Interview Questionnaire

## Business Overview
1. What type of restaurant do you run (cuisine, service style, seating capacity)?
2. How many staff members do you employ (full‑time, part‑time, seasonal)?
3. Which POS / inventory system do you currently use?

## Current Pain Points
4. Which daily/weekly tasks take the most time for you or your staff?
5. Are there decisions you make based on guesswork (e.g., ordering, staffing, pricing)?
6. How do you currently forecast demand or plan inventory?
7. What is the biggest source of waste (food, labor, time)?
8. Have you tried any software tools to address these issues? If so, what was missing?

## Data & AI Readiness
9. Do you collect historical sales, inventory, or reservation data? In what format?
10. How comfortable are you with an AI‑driven recommendation system?
11. What concerns would you have about using AI (accuracy, cost, data privacy, etc.)?

## Financial Impact & Willingness to Pay
12. Roughly how much would you be willing to pay per month for a solution that reduces waste by 10%?
13. What pricing model do you prefer (subscription, per‑order fee, revenue share)?

## Closing
14. Any other challenges you face that we haven’t covered?
15. Would you be interested in a pilot program once we have a prototype?

---
*Feel free to add follow‑up questions during the interview.*
