# Hours
Demo Bistro is open Tuesday to Sunday, 5:00pm to 11:00pm. We are closed on Mondays.
The kitchen stops taking orders at 10:15pm.

# Reservations
We accept reservations for parties of any size. Walk-ins are welcome but seating
is subject to availability. For groups of 8 or more, please book at least 48 hours ahead.

# Dietary & Allergens
We have six vegan dishes and a dedicated gluten-free menu. All allergens are
labelled, and the kitchen can accommodate most dietary restrictions with notice.
Nut-free preparations are available on request.

# Parking
Complimentary valet parking is available from 6:00pm. There is also a public car
park two minutes' walk away on Harbour Street.

# Pets
Well-behaved dogs are welcome on our outdoor terrace, but not in the main dining room.

# Cancellation Policy
Reservations can be cancelled free of charge up to 4 hours before the booking.
Late cancellations for large groups may incur a fee.
